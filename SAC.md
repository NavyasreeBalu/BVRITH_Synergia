# Student Affairs Council (SAC)

## About SAC

The Student Affairs Council (SAC) at BVRIT Hyderabad College of Engineering for Women is the driving force behind Synergia 2026. SAC is dedicated to fostering student engagement, organizing impactful events, and creating memorable experiences that blend innovation, technology, and culture.

## Mission

To empower students through collaborative events that promote creativity, technical excellence, and cultural celebration while building a vibrant campus community.
